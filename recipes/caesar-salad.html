Caesar Salad

Home

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, crunchy croutons, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

This Caesar salad is a perfect side dish or a light meal on its own. The homemade dressing adds a rich flavor, and the croutons provide a satisfying crunch. Enjoy it with grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal!

Ingredients

For the Salad

  • 1 large romaine lettuce (or 2 small), chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup croutons (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
