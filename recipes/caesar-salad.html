Caesar Salad

Home

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, crunchy croutons, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

This Caesar salad is a perfect side dish or a light meal on its own. The homemade dressing adds a rich flavor, and the croutons provide a satisfying crunch. Enjoy it with grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal!

Ingredients

For the Salad

  • 1 large romaine lettuce (or 2 small), chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup croutons (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

For the Dressing

  • 1 egg yolk (optional â€“ can substitute 1 tbsp mayonnaise)
  • 2 anchovy fillets, finely minced (optional)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Make the Dressing: In a medium bowl, whisk together egg yolk, anchovies, garlic, mustard, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce until smooth. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking constantly to emulsify. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Prepare the Salad: Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly. Place lettuce in a large salad bowl.
  3. Assemble: Add croutons and most of the Parmesan to the lettuce. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently until evenly coated. Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan.
  4. Serve Immediately: Best served fresh so the croutons stay crisp.
